Our Idea

We've developed a concept for a small improvement to the home—a parody of a smart home system. This system, built on the Pico W, allows users to monitor temperature, view data on a screen, and control electric appliances. The app is designed to make connecting to Wi-Fi easier for users, while all monitoring and interactions will take place on a website.
